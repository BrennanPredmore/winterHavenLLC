The Challenge

As we approach the end of the business cycle in the midst of the longest economic expansion in history, retail investors will feel the liquidity crunch when the market experiences a downturn or recession defined as a pullback of >30% in the equity markets.
As the realities of the recession make their way to our economy, we will see liquidity contract and when the inevitable layoffs happen, retail investors are forced to liquidate their savings and retirement portfolios, simply to make ends meet week to week.




This leaves them absent during the greatest periods to “buy low.”

Managing risk through properly structured hedges and using post-trade analytical tools is critical. The ability to create optimal trading strategies, back-test them, execute them, and automatically generate reporting data through a unified platform ensures option traders create profitability and maintain real time quantifiable and financial views of their positions and risk exposure.

The Opportunity

The opportunity for improved profits lies within the systematic framework used by the discretionary trader. With the chance to create a single resource including all relevant data, by breaking down silos within the system and through application, execution, and sharing deep expertise up and down the WHC trading community.
