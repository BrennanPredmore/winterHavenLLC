Who we are

David Love
Chief Investment Officer

Summer of 2017 David was Fellow for J.P. Morgan. He is a current economics student at the University of Texas. The founder of Winter Haven Capital, David amassed a trading track record by trading every day since the Trump election in 2016. Through his own merit. David started trading Options and Equities during the Winter 2016 Self-taught through books and real live experience by December 2017 he was managing over $50,000. These funds were given to him by multiple friends eager to capitalize on this opportunity. In addition, David’s accolades throughout his time in college are evident through the internships and part time jobs he’s held in Market Research and Statistical Analysis. His first internship was in 2014 senior year of high school for a startup that was bought for $100+ million and has worked at Avison Young a CRE Market Research firm. In addition, he has experience running marketing campaigns at Emergo which is directly relevant to WHC business model and sales strategy. Finally, Mr. Love’s last role was as a Equity Research Consulting at GQR Capital Markets under co-founder and former Managing Director; Sonny Lo.

Dierk Van Zandt
Chief Technology Officer and Head Architect

Dierk is the Chief Technology Officer at WHC with over 20+ years of technology management and consulting experience, covering buy-side and trading platforms in a broad range of enterprise initiatives. His experience ranges from architecting State Street Global Exchange and Was part of the launch of GovEx, SwapEx, Eris, and the eRates Match electronic trading platforms at eExchange. Dierk holds a BA from the City College of New York and an MBA from the New York University Stern School of Business

Carlos Love
Chief Marketing Officer

Carlos Love has been working in Marketing for nearly 2 decades. Carlos Love earned his bachelor’s degree in computer engineering in Mexico City at Autonomous National University of Mexico in 1984 and earned his MBA from the Technologic Institute of Monterrey in 1992. His finance experience spans from implementing the first trading system in Mexico System as an Engineer for a bank in 1985. In 1996 he transitioned to Financial Consulting at Accenture and worked at IBM as a Financial Services Principal from 2000-2002. In 2003 he was offered a Marketing Manager position at Microsoft where he worked in Miami and Redmond HQ until 2010. In 2011 he became a Sr. Marketing Manager at Dell Technologies.
